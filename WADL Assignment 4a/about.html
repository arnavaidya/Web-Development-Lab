About Us

Our Story

taglio's started in 1995 as a small family-owned pizzeria in downtown. What began as a passion project has grown into the community's favorite pizza destination.

We still make our dough fresh daily and use only the finest ingredients. Every pizza is hand-tossed and baked to perfection in our brick ovens.

Our Commitment

  • Fresh Ingredients

    We source local ingredients whenever possible and prepare everything fresh daily.

  • Traditional Methods

    Our dough is made from a family recipe and allowed to rise for 24 hours.

  • Community Support

    We proudly sponsor local schools and events throughout the year.
